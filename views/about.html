About Us

Get It Logistics is a family owned company, based out of North Texas funded in 2022 with the idea and purpose to help customers reach their goals by providing a top of the line logistics and IT suite of services.


We help your business grow by bringing to the table services such as computer bank life cycle, repairs and erasing data, we also offer warehousing, distribution and kitting.

Get It Logistics has the expertise to tailor the right solution for your needs. Whether you are a small business or a large company, we are here for you. Call or email us today and let us help with your next 3PL project.
